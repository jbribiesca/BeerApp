Burp!

Group 1 - Project 2

Jose, Jerry, Shane, Onix and Nina


Burp! helps you keep track of any beer you have ever tasted.

Wether you are enjoying your beer at the bar, at a friends place or in your own living room, this app will help you remember these moments.

Burp! is all about convenience for beer lovers...which we all are!

No more memorizing the beer names for the ones we tasted in the past, all we have to do is log into the app and look it up in our profile. In addition, we can search any beer to find out its name, type, ABV and brewery. And after we tasted it, we can just rate it and add it to our dashboard. Easy!

The end result of Burp! was only possible with using each of the team members expertise ... which led to some really amazing results.

We realized that most of the work that was done are tied to implementing the ideas into an overall design, reading lots of documentations and debugging/redesign.

As a team we learned that pro active planning, real-time communication and version control are very important factors to succeed working in a team on one outcome.
